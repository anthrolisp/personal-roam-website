Stoic Clock

  • What is it

    • I wanted a way to remind myself of my own humanity, so I built a clock to display not only the current time, but also to serve as a countdown of days till death.
      • days till death = average age @ death of a person of my demographics + my DOB - today's date
    • It sounds morbid, but I find it quite motivating!

  • The mechanics of it

    • A Raspberry Pi powered Nixie Tube Clock.
    • I designed the PCB myself, relying on several online examples
